\# Incident Report: Phishing Alert Investigation



\## Scenario

A medium-severity alert was triggered in the SIEM after a suspicious email bypasssed initial filters. This report documents the end-to-end investigation and escalation process.



\## Actions Taken

1\. \*\*Triage:\*\* Analyzed email headers and extracted a suspicious file hash.

2\. \*\*Analysis:\*\* Used \*\*VirusTotal\*\* and Threat Intelligence to identify the hash as a known Trojan.

3\. \*\*Forensics:\*\* Verified execution on the endpoint through log analysis.

4\. \*\*Escalation:\*\* Documented findings and successfully escalated the incident to L2 for containment.



\## Skills Demonstrated

\* \*\*Tooling:\*\* SIEM, VirusTotal, Sandboxing concepts.

\* \*\*Decision Making:\*\* Accurate severity reassessment from Medium to High.
